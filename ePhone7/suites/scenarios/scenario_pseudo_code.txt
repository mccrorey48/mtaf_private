Scenarios
1. User looks up coworker on contact list and calls by touching contact's call icon
    go_to_contacts_view
        view_buttons_are_displayed
        touch_contacts_button
        contacts_view_appears
        contacts_button_is_highlighted
    go_to_coworkers_list
        contacts_tabs_are_displayed
        touch_coworkers_tab
        coworkers_tab_is_highlighted
    look_up_contact
        scroll_until_contact_is_visible
        contact_call_icon_is_green
    make_call
        touch_contact_call_icon
        call_screen_is_displayed
        call_screen_banner_is_calling
        contact_phone_rings
        contact_answers
        call_screen_banner_is_active_call
        2_way_audio_is_present
    end_call
        touch_end_icon
        call_screen_banner_is_ended
        call_screen_disappears_after_2_seconds
